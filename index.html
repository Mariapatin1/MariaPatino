About Me

A little about who I am

Building things that matter is what gets me out of bed in the morning. As a product manager, I live at the intersection of people and technology — understanding what teams actually need, then making it happen. I've spent the past few years at Apple, where I've shipped internal tools, streamlined engineering workflows, and learned that the best products come from genuinely listening to the people who use them.

Before tech, I ran arts festivals, managed co-working spaces, and worked in retail — all roles that taught me how to read a room, solve problems on the fly, and bring people together around a shared vision. I bring that same energy to every product I build.

When I'm not shipping features, you'll find me exploring new coffee shops, planning my next travel adventure, or tinkering with side projects that probably involve too many spreadsheets.
