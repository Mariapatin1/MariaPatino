About

Programs don't ship themselves — people do. I'm an Engineering Program Manager who thrives in the space between strategy and execution, bringing clarity to complex technical initiatives and momentum to the teams behind them. At Apple, I manage P0 programs across Engineering, Product, and Business teams, building Agile processes from the ground up where none existed before.

My path wasn't traditional. I'm a Year Up alum who started on the Apple Retail floor, worked through operations and community management, and earned my way into engineering program management. That non-linear journey is my superpower — I know how to read a room, solve problems on the fly, and bring people together around a shared goal.

I'm PSM I certified, data-driven in my decision-making, and obsessive about building systems that actually work. Whether it's establishing sprint cadences, creating executive dashboards, or building an entire PM assistant app from scratch, I bring ownership and initiative to everything I do.
